Our Courses

We offer a diverse range of programs to help you achieve your academic and career goals. Explore the courses we offer:

Science +2

In the Medical Science stream, students emphasize Biology, studying the intricacies of living organisms, human anatomy, plant biology, and microbiology. This stream is tailored for those interested in fields such as medicine, nursing, pharmacy, or biotechnology. Students may also choose Mathematics as an elective if they wish to pursue interdisciplinary fields combining biology and technology.The Non-Medical Science stream, on the other hand, focuses more on Mathematics, which is central to fields like engineering, computer science, and applied mathematics. Students in this stream study Physics and Chemistry, preparing them for careers in engineering, architecture, or pure sciences. Many students also opt for Computer Science as an elective, especially those pursuing careers in information technology or software development.

Management +2

In the +2 Management stream, students can choose between Commerce and Computer Science, both of which lay the foundation for future careers in business and technology. The Commerce stream focuses on subjects like Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics (optional), preparing students for degrees like BBA, B.Com, and BMS, with career opportunities in management, finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship. The Computer Science stream combines Mathematics, Computer Science, and Business Studies, equipping students with technical and business knowledge for careers in IT management, software development, and data analysis. Both tracks provide essential skills for students planning to pursue undergraduate courses in business or technology-related fields, such as BBA, BCA, or BMS.

Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

A BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) is an undergraduate degree designed to provide students with a strong foundation in business management and leadership skills. The program typically covers core subjects like Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, Business Communication, and Economics, with a focus on both theoretical knowledge and practical application. Students develop critical thinking, decision-making, and problem-solving skills, which are essential for managing businesses effectively. BBA also helps students build strong interpersonal and communication skills, preparing them for leadership roles in various industries. The course is ideal for those looking to pursue careers in management, marketing, finance, or entrepreneurship. After completing BBA, graduates can further specialize by pursuing a Master of Business Administration (MBA) or other advanced management programs.

Masters Program

A Master of Business Administration (MBA) is a graduate-level program designed to develop advanced management and leadership skills for students aiming for top positions in the business world. The MBA program covers key areas such as Strategic Management, Marketing, Finance, Human Resource Management, and Operations, often with specialized tracks like Entrepreneurship, International Business, or Data Analytics. The curriculum emphasizes critical thinking, decision-making, and problem-solving, helping students navigate complex business environments. MBA graduates are equipped to take on leadership roles in various industries, including corporate management, consulting, finance, and marketing. The program also provides networking opportunities with industry professionals and peers. An MBA can significantly boost career prospects and earning potential, making it a popular choice for those seeking career advancement or entrepreneurship.
