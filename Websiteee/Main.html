About Us

Our Tilottama Campus is a well-known educational institution located in the Rupandehi district of Nepal. It is affiliated with Pokhara University (PU). The campus offers various undergraduate and graduate programs, particularly in fields like business, humanities, and social sciences.Tilottama Campus is named after the ancient city of Tilottama, which is often associated with the area and has cultural significance. The campus is known for providing quality education and fostering an environment conducive to learning, with a strong focus on academic excellence.
